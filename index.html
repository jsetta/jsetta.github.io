“The Little Yellow Car”

is a 1957 BMW Isetta 300 that John Jueckstock bought for $100 as a highschool junior in 1964. It was his first car.

Despite years of cross-country moves and mechanical hijinks ranging from incandescent seats, to a seized piston, to frozen brakes, John has kept this absurd little lemon running all the way to today. Checkout the full story below, and feel free to ask for a ride!
